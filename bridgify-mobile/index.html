How might we design a legal app that helps users identify and address common legal issues they might overlook in their daily lives?

Overview
‍
Simplify legal challenges with features like case exploration, tracking, and an AI legal assistant. It offers a user-friendly interface, plain language guides, and quick access to resources, empowering users to manage legal matters confidently.

CONTEXT

Only 5%-10% of people have Legal APP On Their Phone

Low Adoption for Legal APP

Despite the widespread use of productivity and social media apps, legal apps remain relatively rare on users' devices, with only 5-10% of people having downloaded and used them, highlighting a significant gap in legal tech adoption

RESEARCH

When asking people why they don't have a legal app, they often respond with "Why would I need it?"

Problem

1People often don't realize the legal issues they face in everyday situations, like leases, purchases, or job agreements, leading to missed chances for protection and resolution.

2People often avoid addressing legal issues due to fear of complexity and difficulty in understanding legal language and procedures

Opportunities

1Provide users with resources and information through the app that clearly outline common legal issues they might face.

2Use plain language and user-friendly tools to guide users through legal procedures and decisions, reducing their fear of complexity and improving their confidence in handling legal matters.
